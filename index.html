Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor, Shah Jahan in the year 1653. Taj Mahal is built in the loving memory of Mumtaz Mahal, wife of Emperor Shah Jahan. Taj Mahal is built by 20000 artisans in a span of 22 years. Taj Mahal is one of the famous historical monuments in India.
